Kansas City Ipsum

What...?

After noticing a glaring hole in the lorem-ipsum generator market, KC-Ipsum was born after analyzing 31,482 articles encompassing 9,702,191 words from The Pitch and sprinkling in vocabulary from Wikipedia, BBQ menus, and travel guides. May all of your prototypes and mock-ups represent KC well.

You can check out the github repo to see how this was made and even create your own generator!
